Drawing

Click a shape icon to select a drawing shape.

Click and drag anywhere on the image to begin your drawing.

This is an example of an ellipse shape.

Click on the shape to highlight it.

Change the shapes size by dragging one of the surrounding small white boxes.

Rotate the shape by dragging the small green box.

Delete a shape by clicking on the bin icon.

Remove last shape drawn by clicking on the left arrow icon

Remove all shapes drawn by clicking on the spiral arrow icon

Display the table by clicking on the table icon.

Display a guide by clicking on the info icon.

Table

All drawn shapes are listed here.

Click a row to highlight a shape.

Use the shape icons to filter the table by a shape.

Annotation

The bulge of a galaxy can be marked with a circle. Sometimes there may be more than one bulge!

Use lines to mark a galaxy's spiral arms from the bulge to the end of the arm.

Use an ellipse or circle to outline the boundary of the galaxy.

Tip: If you can spot a rare out of the ordinary feature such as a ring or an irregular galaxy, use your best judgement and try to make it with a shape you think is appropriate. You're ready to Begin!
